The Process – Step 1

Discovery

We kicked off the project with a discovery call where I met all three founders, talked to them about their vision for the company and assessed what they need to make this project a success. We discussed what they needed for their website and their brand. To finalize this step we defined a roadmap and I created a project timeline showing all its stages.

Logo & Visual Identity

Once we were all aligned, the next step was to design the logo and define the visual identity as that would inform all further visual design decisions. We explored a few different directions for the logo and when we selected one of them, we explored further to select brand colors and typeface.


UX/UI – Diana Agronov, Alexey Pavo

Leads – Yulia Leder (UX/UI), Ethan Sharabi (Dev Lead)
